<p>Business Strategies are a type of business driver.  They can appear in hierarchies or in a list with dependencies. Business initiatives should be mapped to the driver that motivates its existence.  (any business initiative not mapped to a driver is not aligned, and the priority of the business initiative should derive from a balance of architectural risk, value of the strategy, and value of the proposed changes).  <br/></p><p><br/></p><p>Business initiatives suggest changes to business processes.  All IT impacts can be found by looking for the system interaction points (process steps that interact with system features).  <br/></p>

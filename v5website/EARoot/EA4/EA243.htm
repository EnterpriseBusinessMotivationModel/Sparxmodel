<p>Starting with the list of architectural risks identified through maturity assessments, a report can be generated to highlight the capabilities with total architectural risks above a specific threshold.  A gap report can be produced if there are no programs or proposed changes targeting those capabilites or if the total investment provided is less than 60% of the total investment required.<br/></p>

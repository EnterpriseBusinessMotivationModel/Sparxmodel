<p>This simple viewpoint illustrates the relationships between business units, processes, metrics and strategies, including the relationship with rules and capabilities.  This is a direct adoption of the connection for business rules from the OMG BMM, which was in turn derived from the work of the business rules group.  Note that this model is not sufficient for full business process modeling. A business process model viewpoint is outside the scope of business architecture, and therefore is not included in the EBMM.<br/></p>

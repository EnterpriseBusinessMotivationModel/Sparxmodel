<p>Customers are the life blood of any commercial enterprise, and this section is rather specifically tailored to commercial organizations.  That said, governmental and non-profit organizations may view their stakeholders as customers, but the relationships are unlikely to be the same.  Further work is taking place on the EBMM to create a view that is better tailored to the kinds of relationships typical in government settings.<br/></p><p><br/></p><p>Terms specific to the customer viewpoint are not terms used by the customer, but rather terms used by the enterprise to understand, communicate with, and develop solutions to the needs of the customer.  The growth of Enterprise Architecture into a richer understanding of customers, their needs and enterprise responses to changes in those needs has been a major focus of EA activity in the years between 2008 and 2014.  <br/></p>

<p>The Primary view illustrates each of the elements that are considered centrral to the EBMM.  It does not show auxiliary elements which are useful for understanding how the EBMM relates to other areas of the business, and it does not include elements that are "taxonomies only" for the sake of clarity. This decision is purely visual and is not reflective of the relative importance of excluded elements.<br/></p><p><br/></p><p>The background areas reflect subsets of elements that "roll up" when looking at the EBMM at a higher level.  The components illustrated with a thick black outline are the core elements in each of these subsets.  Most of the other elements with an area are directly related to that core element.  <br/></p><p><br/></p><p>In the case of business model, the business model is composed of most of the other elements within that area.  In the case of Directive, the other elements are specific instances of directives.  <br/></p>

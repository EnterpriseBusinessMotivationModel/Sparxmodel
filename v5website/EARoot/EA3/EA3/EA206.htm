<p>A Measurement category is a taxonomy applied to process metrics (and therefore, KPIs, which are a type of process metric).  By grouping metrics into categories, methods can be employed to find objectives or initiatives where the success metrics are in opposition to one another (e.g. where the outcome of one objective is to increase a particular measure while the outcome of another is to decrease the same measure).  <br/></p>

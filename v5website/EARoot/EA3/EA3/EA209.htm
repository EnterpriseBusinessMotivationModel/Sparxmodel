<p>A composition of products and/or services (and the prices and conditions under which they are offered), that can be individually purchased by a customer. Offerings may be available only to qualified customers as a way of packaging value for specific types of customers without undercutting other customers. Offerings may also be limited for reasons of distribution, legal concerns, localization, or available organizational or partner competencies.  <br/></p>

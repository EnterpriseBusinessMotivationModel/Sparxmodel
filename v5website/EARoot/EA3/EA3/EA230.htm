<p>A success metric is a measurable value or range of values that a key performance indicator may fall into.  It that describes, in quantifiable terms, the successful outcome of a strategy or business initiative.<br/></p><p><br/></p><p>One type of Success Metric is a CTQ.  A CTQ (Critical to Quality) is the measureable aspect of the customer experience that is addressed by a process improvement project.  The CTQs are identified as a result of gathering the Voice of the Customer.<br/></p><p><br/></p><p>alias: Critical To Quality (CTQ), Customer Input KPI, Success Metric, Target Measure<br/></p>

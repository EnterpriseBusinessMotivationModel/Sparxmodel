<p>The work products of value streams, Business Transactions represent a measurable exchange of value either within or between organizations.  A business transaction is frequently the trigger for one or more additional processes.  Information about business transactions is normally managed in a data object.  Examples include Invoice, Sales Order, Purchase Order, Shipping Manifest, Credit Memo, etc.<br/></p>

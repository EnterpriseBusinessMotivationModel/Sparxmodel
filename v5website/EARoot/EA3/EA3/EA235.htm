<p>A tool feature is an abstract representation of the ability of a tool to meet the specific needs of a business process at a system interaction point.  Features are describable, testable, and perceivable by the users of the tool.  Application features tend to be information-oriented while physical tools (like machines, vehicles, or even furniture) tends to be oriented to the physical measurement, management, manipulation, or manufacturing of an item.<br/></p>

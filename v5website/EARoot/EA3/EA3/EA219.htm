<p>This element of the business model contains a description of the specific products and/or services offered by the business. It is important to recognize that the specific products or services developed must derive from customer demands in order to effectively provide value. This relationship, between customers demands and the products offered, is the central focus of marketing in many organizations.<br/></p>

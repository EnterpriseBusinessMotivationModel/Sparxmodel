<p>A governance body is an empowered group of individuals, within an enterprise, with the right to make decisions about the policies, efforts, and/or business rules of an enterprise function, capability, or process.  Governance bodies are typically chartered in a formal manner with specific responsibilities, which may include<br/></p><p><br/></p><p><ul><li>oversight of investment to produce specific results</li><br/></p><p><li>oversight of operations to maintain controls on cost, speed, and/or quality</li><br/></p><p><li>oversight of planning functions to manage change functions</li><br/></p><p><li>oversight of market positioning / mergers / competitive awareness</li><br/></p><p></ul><br/></p><p>While some governance bodies may be chartered to operate continuously, other bodies may be formed at the beginning of a project or initiative and be disbanded after that effort is completed.  <br/></p>

<p>A required measurable attribute of software that describes a desired level of quality for a system.  These are requirements to meet performance levels of Availability, Efficiency, Flexibility, Integrity, Interoperability, Maintainability, Portability, Reliability. Reusability, Robustness, Testability, and Usability.  These attributes are known as System Quality Attributes by the SEI ATAM method.<br/></p><p><br/></p><p>Selecting the relative priority of measurable attributes for a single system often requires tradeoffs.  These priorities are best set by the system's customer.<br/></p>

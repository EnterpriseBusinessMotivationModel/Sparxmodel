<p>A project can be defined as a temporary endeavor undertaken to create a unique product or service.<br/></p><p><br/></p><p>Projects are different from other ongoing operations in an organization because unlike operations, projects have a definite beginning and an end - they have a limited duration.<br/></p><p>Projects are critical to the realization of performing organization's business strategy because projects are a means by which the initiatives of the company are implemented.<br/></p><p><br/></p><p>Projects typically involve doing or creating unique outcomes, and are therefore each one is unique. A product or service may be unique even if the category to which it belongs is large. For example, although several residential complexes have been built in the past, creation of a new house will be a project because each individual facility is unique - different location, design, resources working, owners etc.<br/></p><p>(Source PMI)<br/></p>

A vision is a statement or compound group of statements that describes the desired state of success for an enterprise. It is often described from the viewpoint of the customers or stakeholders. For example, a retailer of electronics could describe their vision as follows: "to be the most trusted, reliable, and cost-competitive source of electronic products in the Greater Seattle area."  <br/><br/>Alternative definition from the OMG Business Motivation Model:<br/>A Vision describes the future state of the enterprise, without regard to how it is to be achieved.   <br/>A Vision is the ultimate, possibly unattainable, state the enterprise would like to achieve.  A Vision is often compound, rather than focused toward one particular aspect of the business problem.  <p/>

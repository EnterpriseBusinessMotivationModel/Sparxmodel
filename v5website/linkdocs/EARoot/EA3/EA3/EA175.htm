A management tool, usually presented as a report, that illustrates a set of carefully chosen metrics designed to provide useful feedback on the health and alignment of the enterprise.  One type of business scorecard is a "Balanced Scorecard," referring to a concept developed by Drs. Robert Kaplan (Harvard Business School) and David Norton. <br/>A Balanced Scorecard enables a Strategy to be broken down into Key Performance Indicators. Performance against the KPIs is used to demonstrate how well the Strategy is being achieved. A Balanced Scorecard has 4 major areas, each of which has a small number of KPIs. The same 4 areas are considered at different levels of detail throughout the Organisation.<p/>

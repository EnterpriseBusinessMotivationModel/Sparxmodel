One set of stakeholders for the EBMM is the IT program itself.  For those IT planning stakeholders that wish to reflect the connection between business models, capabilities, drivers, processes and requirements, this view is helpful. The entire "design" view is a subset of this viewpoint.  <br/><br/>The key difference between this viewpoint and the planning viewpoint is that it is used to illustrate the traceability of IT efforts with the pivot being the IT effort itself (Applications and Requirements).  In effect, the need to demonstrate traceability is often an exercise in justification.  Reports and models aligned with this viewpoint are sufficient to demonstrate that justification.<p/>

The planning viewpoint illustrates the elements relevant to the needs of business units (and usually their IT groups) accountable for taking the drivers and producing a roadmap that illustrates how the business will change to meet those needs.  Normally, the needs of alignment can be met using views from this viewpoint.<p/>

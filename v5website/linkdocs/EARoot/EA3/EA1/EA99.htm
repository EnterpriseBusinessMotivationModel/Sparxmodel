One set of stakeholders for the EBMM is the IT program itself.  For those IT programs that wish to reflect the connection between business drivers, processes and requirements, this view is helpful.  Note that this viewpoint is overly simplistic.  That is intentional to make life easier for IT architects and engineers.  It is the experience of this model's author that the value of connecting any of the IT elements to business architecture elements other than the listed ones produces diminishing returns at best.<p/>

Strategies are the real motivators of business behavior.  This model is an indirect derivative of the OMG Business Motivation Model.  This illustration puts strategy into the context of other elements within the model.<br/><br/>A view produced in accordance with this viewpoint would illustrate the relationships between the various elements of motivation, normally to demonstrate alignment between a proposed change and all of the drivers and influencers that demand that change to occur.  <br/><br/>Note that drivers do not have a direct relationship to directives.  That is because the decision to implement a driver is independent of the driver itself. In other words, a policy ignored is still a policy.  The decision to implement it would require a business objective, and from that, a success metric to be defined.<p/>

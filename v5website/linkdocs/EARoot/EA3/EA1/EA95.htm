This viewpoint exists to illustrate how ERP implementations can benefit from the EBMM metamodel.  In an ERP implementation, a capability analysis can indicated the changes to business processes, but after that, business process is the key. You work to match the needs of the process to the "capabilties" of the ERP system.  (The EBMM term is "Application Feature" but for this view, we are using an alias of "Application Capability" to improve understanding for this stakeholder community).<p/>
